# Signal — Steam Games Analysis (May 2026)

A data-driven analysis of 10,000 Steam games, examining what players 
actually value — and what the data reveals about the gaming industry's 
biggest assumptions.

## Report Findings
- The $10-20 price range produces the highest rated games on Steam
- Established live service games retain 45-80% of peak players — new entrants retain almost nothing
- 1,777 games qualify as hidden gems — loved by players, invisible to most
- Not a single AAA publisher appears in Steam's top 10 best reviewed games
- SEGA is the only major publisher consistently delivering quality at scale

## Methodology
- **Dataset:** 10,000 Steam games
- **Sources:** SteamSpy API, Steam Web API, SteamDB
- **Tools:** Python, pandas, matplotlib
- **Date:** May 2026
